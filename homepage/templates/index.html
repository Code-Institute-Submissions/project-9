Stories:

Amanda Simmings

When I ran away from home I was only twenty years old, and scared for my life. I ran because my father was beating me daily, being careful not leave any marks that people could see. My Mum never even tried to stop him, she would say it was my fault or I deserved it. So i ran and never looked back as i knew if i stayed i probably be dead by now.

When Help the Homeless found me I was close to giving up hope that my life would improve and was considering ending it. But now after being given a place to stay and training so that i could get a job, I have become a social services officer to help support other women who suffer from domestic violence and being abused.

James Conway

Before I became homeless I was a successful businessmen with my own family run DIY shop in our local town. But with the big corporations taking up all our customers we eventually went bankrupt. with massive debt and no form of income everything was taken away from me, I had lost my wife and my home. With no one to turn to for help I ended up living on the streets begging for money so that i could at least feed myself now and then.

Thanks to the support of Help the Homeless I am in the progress of putting my life back together, I have a roof over my head and am doing courses in IT and accounting whilst working a part time job. If it was not for them i would still be sleeping rough trying to survive.

Laura Hockley

I was once a school teacher before becoming homeless. One day after class one of my student came you to me and tried to blackmail me with false information. When I wouldn't do as he asked the boy started spreading rumors about myself and another student. Everything was down hill from there. my friends and family would not have anything to do with me I had no money due to the legal cost from court and eventually lost my home and job.

Help the homeless were there for me when no one else was. They provided me with a room to stay in and meals daily, and even investigated what happened to me. There investigation revealed the boy who started the rumors had blackmailed several other teachers in the past and reported this to the police. This lead to his arrest and my re-en statement as a teacher and a generous compensation for what happened.

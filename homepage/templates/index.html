Our Goals

Housing

Our main goal is to provide as many homeless people as we can a safe place to sleep at night. Every year many die due to exposure to the harse weather and we feel this is unacceptable. To combat this situation we aim to aquire propertise we can use to shelter those at risk.

Training

For many of these unfortunate indaviduals becoming homeless has been due to not being able to retrain for new jobs. We aim to try and provide any and all nessissary training through collage courses to help those we take in to learn new skill sets that could help them gain employment.

General Support

Just housing and training is not enough, funding to help support the basic needs of those we try to help is crucial. From food to transport fee's it is important we help support them until they are able to support themselves financially.

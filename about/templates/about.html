About Us

Help the Homeless is a none profit organisation dedicated to improving the lives of those struggling to rebuild after everyone else has given up on them. We aim to help lower the amount of people living on the streets, provide training so that they can gain employment and support there daily needs until such time that they are once again able to support themselves. Each year more and more young and old people are forced to live in terrible conditions because they have nowhere else to go. for many of the young who are living rough the potential for a better life goes ignored, either because mo one wants to take them in or provide them with the means to better take care of themselves. That is why we were founded, like many people out there we wish to help, to this end we decided it was time someone stood up and said no more!
